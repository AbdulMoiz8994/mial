📲 2. End-to-End User Journey Flow
Here’s the high-level UX flow with screen progression 👇
1️⃣ Welcome / Sign Up  →  2️⃣ Onboarding Questions (Business Type + Goals)  →  3️⃣ AI Generates Content Plan  →  4️⃣ Dashboard: Plan Overview  →  5️⃣ Post Editor / Customisation (AI Captions + Brand Styling)  →  6️⃣ Scheduling & Publishing Calendar  →  7️⃣ Analytics & Recommendations  →  8️⃣ Upgrade / Subscription Screen


🧩 3. Screen-by-Screen UI Breakdown
🟪 1. Welcome / Sign-Up
Goal: Quick entry, personality, excitement.
UI Layout:
Animated splash (“Hi, I’m MIA 💬 – your intelligent content agent.”)
Buttons:
Continue with Google
Continue with Email
Small note: “7-day free trial. No credit card required.”
Light gradient background (lavender, blush, or gold tones).
Minimal AI-inspired line pattern / flowing gradients for energy.
Microinteraction: Button press → “👋 Let’s get you started” animation.
🟣 2. Onboarding – Personalisation
Goal: Capture context for AI engine.
Screen 1:
“What type of business are you?”
Buttons: 💇 Hair Salon / 💅 Nail Bar / 💈 Barber / 💆 Spa / Other.
Continue →
Screen 2:
“What are your goals?” (multi-select chips)
“Get more clients”
“Post consistently”
“Build brand awareness”
“Grow followers”
Screen 3:
“Upload your logo + choose your brand colours.”
Upload Logo (with preview)
Color picker or pre-generated palettes.
Optional font style preview (Serif / Sans / Handwritten).
CTA → “Generate My Content Plan” (AI loads).

🟩 3. AI Content Plan Screen
Goal: WOW moment — instant value.
Layout:
Header: “✨ Your 7-Day Smart Plan is Ready!”
Card grid (each day = 1 content idea):
Day 1: “Before & After Transformation” → auto-caption + image suggestion.
Day 2: “Client Testimonial Post.”
Day 3: “Hair Care Tip Carousel.”
Bottom CTA:
Customize Plan
Approve & Continue
Microinteraction: AI typing animation (“Generating post ideas…”).
🟦 4. Dashboard / Home
Goal: Control center for ongoing content.
Sections:
Header: Greeting (“Good afternoon, Maya 👋”)
Next Post Scheduled: Large card with thumbnail, time, edit button.
Quick Actions Row:
➕ New Post
✨ AI Idea
📅 Calendar
📊 Insights
Content Performance Summary:
Engagement overview bar (likes/comments/reach)
AI Tip: “Your clients loved the hair care videos last week — post more of that!”
Sidebar (mobile collapsible): Home, Calendar, Library, Analytics, Settings.
🟧 5. Post Editor / Customisation
Goal: Personalize, polish, brandify.
Layout:
Top Bar: Back | Save Draft | Publish.
Main Canvas (Canva-style):
Center: Post preview (image/video).
Left Panel: Layers (Text, Image, Shape).
Right Panel:
AI Caption field (editable).
Hashtag generator.
Brand colors & logo (auto-applied).
Button: Regenerate Caption | Suggest Hashtags.
Bottom Toolbar: Upload media, add text overlay, templates library.
Pre-Designed Template Categories:
“Before & After”
“Promotional Offer”
“Quote of the Day”
“Client Testimonial”
“Hair Tip Carousel”
Microinteraction: Drag-and-drop stickers, smooth snapping grid, undo/redo.
🟨 6. Scheduling & Publishing
Goal: Plan + automate content effortlessly.
UI Layout:
Top Tabs: Calendar | Queue | Drafts.
Calendar View:
Color-coded posts per platform (IG purple, FB blue, TikTok black).
Tap → open detail drawer (caption, image, edit, reschedule).
Add New Post: Floating button bottom-right.
Manual Posting Option:
Toggle: “Auto-post” / “Remind Me to Post.”
Push notification reminder setup.
Micro UX: Drag post card → drop on calendar date → auto-schedules.
🟫 7. Analytics & Insights
Goal: Empower salon owners to understand what works.
Layout:
Header: “This Week’s Highlights ✨”
Metrics Row:
Total Posts
Avg Engagement
Follower Growth
Top Performing Post (thumbnail + stats).
Charts:
Engagement Over Time (line chart)
Platform Breakdown (donut chart)
AI Insight Cards:
“Post more blonding reels on Thursdays 🌞”
“Your testimonial posts perform 2× better than others.”
Microinteraction: Hover → metric details tooltip (“Reach: 2.1k | +15% WoW”).
🟪 8. Subscription / Billing
Goal: Upsell naturally.
Layout:
Trial Countdown Banner: “Your free trial ends in 3 days – unlock unlimited posts.”
Pricing Cards:
Basic – 20 posts/month + AI captions.
Pro – Unlimited posts + analytics.
Salon Team – Multi-location, staff logins, shared templates.
Highlight “Pro” as recommended (gradient border + crown icon 👑).
Integrated Stripe/PayPal pop-up.
